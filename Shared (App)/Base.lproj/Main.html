Bonjourr

Instructions

MacOS

Here is how to manually enable Bonjourr on Safari MacOS:

  1. Open Safari settings window by going to Safari > Settings... or pressing CMD + ,.
  2. In the settings, go to the Extensions tab, and enable Bonjourr Startpage.
  3. Lastly, back to the General tab, verify that New tabs open: and New windows open: are set to Bonjourr - Minimalist Startpage
